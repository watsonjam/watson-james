Mzumbe secondary school joining instruction 2022 – Mzumbe secondary school

Mzumbe Secondary School is one of the greatest secondary school in the country. We are a school with an exciting and rigorous learning environment for both students and staff. Our staff remains committed in all that we do to uphold the high standards that have earned our school several awards and honors. As a result, the school has been named among the highest-performing high schools in the country. We provide a strong educational foundation for our students with the help of an amazing group of teachers, administrators, and supporting staff.

Form one joining instruction 2022 is a document that show selected for admission into secondary schools. Your Form one joining instruction contains information about the school you were selected, necessary requirements, tuition fee, other fees and everything that you should bring along with during the reporting time.
